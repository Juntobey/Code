Alt-J (stylized as alt-J, real name Î”) is an indie rock band from England, formed in 2007 in Leeds.
The band consists of Joe Newman (guitar/lead vocals), Thom Sonny Green (drums), Gus Unger-Hamilton (keyboards/vocals), and formerly Gwil Sainsbury (guitar/bass).

Their first album, An Awesome Wave, was released in May 2012 in Europe and in September 2012 in the United States.
It won the 2012 British Mercury Prize. Sainsbury left the band in early 2014.
